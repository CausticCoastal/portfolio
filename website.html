I have been working with creatives of all types for over a decade, imagining ways to showcase what they do.

What began as a need to document the artistic projects I was making, grew into a fascination for exploring the processes and ideas surrounding the way artists make work.

Each film is an exploration of imagination and curiosity that serves to tell a rich story about a moment in time.

My work has spanned collaborations with artists, musicians, fashion designers, brands, and fellow filmmakers, always exploring new ways to create visual stories that resonate.
